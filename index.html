About Us

Welcome to Politeknik Negeri Bandung (POLBAN), a leading polytechnic institution in Indonesia committed to delivering high-quality education and fostering innovation. Established in 1995 as Politeknik ITB, POLBAN has consistently aimed to produce skilled professionals who can contribute significantly to various industries and society. Our campus is home to a diverse community of students and faculty, dedicated to academic excellence and practical learning. We offer a wide range of programs in engineering, technology, and applied sciences, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern workforce. Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on training, research opportunities, and collaboration with industry partners, providing students with real-world experience and skills.

At POLBAN, we believe in the importance of lifelong learning and continuous improvement. We strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and critical thinking. Our commitment to research and community engagement further strengthens our role as a catalyst for positive change in society.

Join us at Politeknik Negeri Bandung, where education meets excellence and innovation drives success.
